About Low Speed Vehicles

Low Speed Vehicles (LSVs) are electric vehicles that can travel at speeds of 20-25 mph. They are street legal on roads with posted speed limits of 35 mph or less in most states. LSVs offer an eco-friendly, cost-effective transportation alternative for neighborhoods, planned communities, campuses, and small towns.

All street-legal LSVs include required safety features such as headlights, turn signals, mirrors, windshield, seat belts, and more. They provide convenient transportation with lower operating costs than traditional vehicles.
